About



Since childhood, I have always been curios as to how technologies really worked, and often tried to tinker around to see how things would change. Whenever faced with a problem, I would simply search for an answer online and find one within minutes. This has allowed me to explore the computing world and has made it a habit for me to constantly learn new things on a daily basis.



Upon graduating from high school, I was awarded a scholarship to study Computer Science in the United Kingdom. I did my CS degree in the University of Strathclyde, during of which I have had the opportunity to both learn and apply a wide range of programming skills and techniques. During my final year, I developed a spell checker for the Kuwaiti Arabic dialect using Machine Learning. Now that I have successfully graduated with a First Class Honurs, I hope to kickstart my career as a Software Engineer in Kuwait.
